Carbonara

Description

We all know italians make good food, pizzas, pasta... yeah good stuff. One of my favourites is Carbonara, a tasty mix of egg, pasta, pepper, cheesee and bacon. The truth is that the original recipe doesn't use bacon, italians did use the cheek of the pig, what they call the "guanciale".

Today we'll learn how to make this delicious food in a few steps, and a really short amount of time. Do you need to stay coding for 12 hours but you need to eat something? Make some CARBONARA and everything will be better in your life (and your code)

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Spaghetti are the most common but use whatever you want :P )
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesian cheesee
  • 2 eggs (We will only use the yolk)
  • bacon (Use guanciale if you have some )

Depending of your tastes or type of meat we will need either salt and olive oil

Steps

Now that we have everything to start and a masive feeling of starving, let's cook!

  1. Start boiling some water, once is boiling you can add some salt and oil, then the pasta, or just the pasta
  2. While the pasta is boiling, cut in small rectangles the bacon or the guanciale
  3. Put a pan in the fire, if you're using bacon add some oil, just a little
  4. If you're using guanciale put it directly into the pan, the same fat of the guanciale will do the work of the oil
  5. High fire for the meat for around the same time the pasta boils( around 8 mins depending of the pasta
  6. Make sure you move time to time the meat or it will burn
  7. Once the pasta is done. grab a glass or a cup and save some boiling water for later
  8. Strain the pasta then cut the boiling of the pasta putting it under cold water
  9. Mix the meat and the pasta and move it
  10. Meanwhile separate the egg white of the yolk
  11. Mix some parmesian with the yolk and stir until you get a homogenius mix, add cheesse if you need to
  12. Add the egg sauce and the aforementioned water to the pan with the pasta and the meat and stir until is mixed
  13. Serve it on a plate and add black pepper
  14. Done! Enjoy eating!
